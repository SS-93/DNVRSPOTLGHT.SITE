2026 Spotlight Awards

Celebrating the visionaries, creators, and community leaders shaping the future of Denver's cultural landscape.

🏆 Award Categories

Artist of the Year

Recognizing outstanding creative achievement and community impact.

Emerging Talent

Highlighting newcomers who have made significant waves this year.

Venue Excellence

Honoring spaces that provide the best platform for local talent.

Community Leader

For those driving social change through the arts.

Best Collaboration

Celebrating the power of collective creativity.

Digital Innovator

Excellence in media, tech, and digital storytelling.

📅 Timeline
May 1 - 15
Nominations Open: Submit your favorite artists and venues for consideration.
June 1 - 20
Public Voting: The community casts their final ballots for the top nominees.
July 12
Awards Gala: Winners announced live at the Denver Spotlight Connects event.
⚖️ Rules & Eligibility
  • Must be a resident or organization based in the Denver Metro area.
  • One vote per verified MediaID DNA profile to ensure fairness.
  • Nominees must have been active within the last 12 calendar months.
  • Self-nominations are permitted and encouraged.
  • Final results are audited by the Denver Spotlight Board of Directors.
